# Backstory

Gnome · Mage · 87 years old

🐑 Shifu | 💧 Bob | ⚔️ Martin

## Appearance

A calming presence. He moves with surprising agility for his age. His features are soft, his gaze often wandering elsewhere. He wears modest, comfortable clothes, always neat but never ostentatious.
His impeccably organized backpack always lets out a thin, suspicious trail of smoke, as if everything he carries is on the verge of exploding.

## Habits and way of life

His manners are impeccable, almost old-fashioned. He speaks calmly and kindly. He has a slightly idealized view of the world, paired with a sincere interest in small details.

He enjoys tinkering, reading, and improving small everyday objects. He will gladly spend hours optimizing something no one else would have thought needed improvement.

He appreciates quiet moments, especially sitting with someone, sharing tea, and talking at length about everything and nothing.

He spends a great deal of time among the Kalu'ak people, and still returns to them often to this day. When he was younger, he used to spend long days by the water fishing at their side, memories he still recalls with great tenderness.

He also sometimes conjures lavish tables for his loved ones. He is immensely proud of them, describing refined dishes in precise detail... even if, in practice, the results sometimes leave something to be desired, though everyone politely pretends to enjoy them.

## Martin, the poor squire

His squire often looks both resigned and slightly worried, knowing that his master's call usually means an unpleasant task that rarely goes according to plan.

## Shifu the unflappable

Found in Elwynn Forest, Yüki accidentally cursed him during one of his very first experiments. Ever since, the poor sheep has been doomed to relive his tragic death over and over again.

## Bob the enthusiastic

An elemental of astonishing power, but completely uncontrollable. Yüki very rarely uses his spells for combat, preferring to let Bob handle the situation. He attacks indiscriminately, spreading cheerful chaos across the battlefield. Forced to apologize after every battle, Yüki describes him as very willing, just a little too enthusiastic.

## Questionable dreams...

While studying a book about dimensional rifts, he misread certain instructions when he decided to "run a test." Since then, he has had strikingly lucid dreams that sometimes seem to leave subtle demonic traces in reality. Out of fear, he prefers to ignore these phenomena and greatly downplay them.

## Companions

**Shifu:** A cursed sheep condemned to relive his death for eternity.
**Bob:** A water elemental as loyal as he is unpredictable.
**Martin:** A devoted squire, always ready to help, often exposed to the consequences of his master's experiments.

## General impression

Yüki gives the impression of a calm, charming, almost ordinary gnome. In some of his slips or blunders, he can briefly seem to conceal considerable power, though one that he appears completely unaware of, which is probably for the best.

## Recent origin

During a stay in Kul Tiras, Yüki met fishermen connected to the maritime routes of the Alcédis family. Over the course of their conversations, he discovered a discreet yet prestigious company made up of sailors, merchants, and explorers who were few in number but highly seasoned. That is how, almost by chance, he came to consider following them, driven more by a taste for adventure than by ambition.
