# RP Backstory

Gnome · Mage · 87 years old

🐑 Shifu | 💧 Bob | ⚔️ Martin

## Appearance

Small but with a soothing presence. His features are gentle, his gaze often elsewhere. Modest and comfortable clothes, always clean but never ostentatious. His impeccably organized backpack always lets out a thin suspicious smoke, as if everything he carries were two seconds away from exploding. He moves with surprising agility for his age.

## Temperament

Impeccably polite, almost old-fashioned. He speaks calmly and kindly, taking the time to choose his words. He has a slightly idealized view of the world, with a sincere interest in the small details others ignore — and an irrepressible need to optimize them to excess. Where others see a minor inconvenience, he sees something to improve.

## Traits

- Humble, never seeks to impress, but tends to embellish his stories in a charming way
- Ingenious and creative
- Extreme optimization
- Attentive to details, sometimes excessively so
- Hardworking and diligent

## Habits and passions

Yükisan leads a simple life, shaped by his routines.

He likes tinkering, reading, and improving small everyday objects. He will gladly spend hours optimizing something no one else would have considered necessary.

He enjoys quiet moments, especially sitting with someone, sharing tea, and talking at length about everything and nothing.

He spends a great deal of time among the Kalu'ak people, and still returns there often today. When he was younger, he spent long days by the water, memories he still speaks of with great tenderness.

He also sometimes conjures lavish tables for those close to him. He is extremely proud of it, describing refined dishes with precision... even if, in practice, the result sometimes leaves something to be desired, everyone always pretends to enjoy it out of politeness.

## Martin, always in the wrong place

His squire often looks both resigned and slightly worried — knowing that his master's call usually means an unpleasant task that rarely goes as planned.

## Bob the enthusiast

An elemental of astonishing power, but completely uncontrollable. Yüki very rarely uses his spells for combat, preferring to let Bob handle the situation. He attacks indiscriminately, spreading joyful chaos across the battlefield. Forced to apologize after every battle, Yüki describes him as very willing, but a little too enthusiastic.

## Questionable dreams...

While studying a book on dimensional rifts, he misinterpreted certain instructions when he wanted to "run a test." Since a mishandling of a book on dimensional rifts, he has had lucid dreams that sometimes leave subtle demonic traces in reality. He prefers to ignore these phenomena and greatly downplay them.

## Companions

**Shifu :** An immortal sheep who almost always stays by his side, surprisingly unfazed by danger.

**Bob :** A water elemental as loyal as he is unpredictable.

**Martin :** A devoted squire, always ready to help, often exposed to the consequences of his master's experiments.

## Overall impression

Yüki gives the impression of a quiet, charming, almost ordinary gnome. In some of his slips or awkward moments, he can briefly seem to conceal considerable power, but one that completely escapes his control. And that is probably for the best.

## Recent origins

During a stay in Kul Tiras, Yükisan met fishermen connected to the maritime routes of the Alcédis family. Through their exchanges, he discovered a discreet yet prestigious company made up of sailors, merchants, and explorers who were few in number but seasoned. Thus, almost by chance, he came to consider following them — more out of a taste for encounters than out of ambition.
